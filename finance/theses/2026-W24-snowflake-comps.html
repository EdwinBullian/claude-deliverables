• Comps imply ~$200 (peer-median EV/Rev) to ~$335 (75th pct) — strikingly ABOVE the strict DCF base ($94). This relative-vs-intrinsic gap IS the thesis.

• Gross margin (~67%) is structurally below pure-SaaS peers because Snowflake resells underlying hyperscaler compute — a permanent governor on terminal margins.
• Snowflake's RPO growth (+38% YoY to $9.21B) re-accelerated above the +30% threshold this quarter — the leading indicator most supportive of the consumption re-acceleration the multiple needs.
