SCENARIO NARRATIVES
BULL
Arsenal-1 hyperscales autonomous production (Fury/Roadrunner/Barracuda + classified); the $20B Army enterprise ceiling and CCA production convert to obligated revenue; Anduril becomes the software-defined prime. FY31E revenue ~$26B at 23% steady EBIT margin, WACC 9.5%. Implied ~$82 — still below the $100 mark, i.e. even the bull barely reaches the Series H price.
BASE
Anduril delivers the $4.3B 2026E guide, scales to ~$20B revenue by 2031 (~45% 6-yr CAGR) and reaches a 19% steady-state EBIT margin as Arsenal-1 volume absorbs fixed cost. WACC 12%, terminal g 3.5%. Implied ~$29 (-71% vs mark). Nearly all value is terminal — early FCF is negative on the guided ~$1.2B 2026 operating loss + factory capex.
BEAR
Program timing slips, procurement reform stalls, and autonomous-systems margins stay compressed by hardware content and price-to-win pressure. Revenue reaches only ~$10B by 2031 (~31% CAGR) at a 14% terminal margin; WACC 14%. Implied ~$10 — the operating business is worth little more than the post-Series-H cash.
