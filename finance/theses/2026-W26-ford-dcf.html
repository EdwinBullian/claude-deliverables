SCENARIO NARRATIVES
BULL
BULL: Ford Pro scales high-margin software/services (paid subs >2M), Model e reaches breakeven by 2028 on the low-cost UEV platform, tariffs ease, and the stock re-rates toward ~6x EV/EBITDA as through-cycle adj. EBIT margin holds ~6%. Implied ~$28.
BASE
BASE: Mature ~1.7% revenue CAGR; consolidated adj. EBIT margin recovers from 3.6% (2025) toward ~5.3% as Pro stays strong and Model e losses shrink (but stay negative). Capex ~5.8% of revenue keeps FCF conversion modest. WACC 12%, g 1.5%. Implied ~$17 — modestly above spot.
BEAR
BEAR: US auto demand rolls over, pricing/mix normalize, Model e losses persist, tariffs bite. Margin compresses to ~3.5-4%; WACC 13.5% on cyclicality. Implied ~$10 — near the 52-week low. Dividend likely trimmed in a true downturn.
