SCENARIO NARRATIVES
BULL
BULL ($68): Higher-for-longer rate curve keeps NIM ~2.1-2.2%, loan & deposit growth compounds, efficiency ratio falls below 60%, and ~$30B+ annual buybacks at ~2x TBV stay accretive - ROTCE holds ~16%. Discounted at 9.5% cost of equity, 4.5% terminal growth.
BASE
BASE ($47): NII tailwind fades as the curve normalizes; ROTCE fades from 16% toward ~13% by FY31 as deposit costs catch up and fee growth moderates. Payout ~75%. Cost of equity 10.05% (beta 1.15), terminal growth 4.0%. Implies ~1.6x TBVPS - below the 1.95x the market pays today.
BEAR
BEAR ($27): The Fed cuts, NIM compresses below 2.0%, CRE and card credit normalize above through-cycle, and loan growth stalls - ROTCE falls to ~10-11%. Cost of equity 11.0%, terminal growth 3.5%. Re-rates toward ~1.0-1.1x TBVPS.
