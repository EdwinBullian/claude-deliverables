Counter-Intuitive Take

The OpenAI revenue miss is a buy signal for hyperscalers — not a sell signal.

The market read OpenAI's internal revenue shortfall as bearish for AI broadly. Wrong lesson. OpenAI is the best-funded AI company in the world, with $852B valuation and $122B fresh capital — and even they struggle to generate revenue fast enough to pay their compute bills. That means AI compute demand is real, structural, and supply-constrained. The infrastructure providers (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, Nvidia) are price-setters in a genuinely scarce market. OpenAI's struggle is the proof.

Thesis through-line: AI application layer = margin compression ahead. AI infrastructure layer (MSFT Azure, AWS, NVDA, Databricks) = where defensible value is accreting.
